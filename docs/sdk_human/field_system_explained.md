# Field System Explained (Human Guide)

## What are fields?

Fields are invisible mathematical forces spread through the whole scene.
Think of them like wind, waves, or energy zones.

Instead of editing each object one-by-one, fields influence everything at once.

## What do they visually do?

- **Noise Field**: makes worlds feel organic and alive.
- **Flow Field**: creates directional movement or drift.
- **Pulse Field**: sends expanding energy waves through the environment.
- **Audio Field**: ties motion/intensity to beat-like bands.

## Why this is useful

You can build huge scenes with simple JSON:

- generator creates structure
- fields add life and motion
- timeline animates both

No textures, no external assets.

## How fields interact with generators

Examples:

- Circuit world traces brighten as pulse waves pass.
- Tunnel rings breathe as noise/flow values change.
- Fractal temple pillars subtly morph with spatial energy.

## Scene ideas

- **Prime Pulse Chamber**: pulse + audio field + temple generator.
- **Psytrance Corridor**: tunnel generator + flow/noise fields.
- **Living Circuit Ocean**: circuit generator + pulse + kick-reactive audio.

## Quick authoring tip

Start with one field and one generator. Confirm mood. Then layer timeline keyframes.
