# How Demos Transition (Human SDK)

Aurex-X transitions now morph scenes procedurally rather than hard-cutting.

## Creative transition styles
- **PulseFlash**: energetic flash for drops
- **PatternDissolve**: masked dissolve through pattern identity
- **FractalZoom**: zoom into detail to reveal next world
- **HarmonicSmear**: musical stretch between moments
- **CathedralBloom**: architectural reveal bloom
- **RhythmStutter**: beat-synced stutter cuts for high energy

## Auto direction
Set a transition to `auto` to let director rules pick style/duration/intensity from scene structure and audio intensity.

## Authoring tip
Use explicit `spec` for iconic moments, `auto` for connective tissue between blocks.
