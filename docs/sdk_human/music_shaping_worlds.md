# Music Shaping Worlds (Human Guide)

In Aurex-X, sound can literally reshape the scene.

## What harmonic mode does

- Bass can widen/twist geometry.
- Melody can drive spiral particle motion.
- High frequencies can make materials flicker and glow.

## Practical workflow

1. Pick a generator (tunnel/temple/particles).
2. Add `harmonics.geometry` to connect bass or chords to structure.
3. Use `SpectralReactive` material for frequency color pulses.
4. Add `harmonics.particles` for musical particle choreography.

## Quick scene pattern

- **Geometry:** `Bass`
- **Materials:** `High`
- **Particles:** `Melody`

This usually gives clear, musical visual contrast.

## Tips

- Keep strengths near `0.6-1.3` first.
- If visuals are too chaotic, lower particle strength before lowering geometry.
- For calmer scenes, bind materials to `Mid` instead of `High`.
