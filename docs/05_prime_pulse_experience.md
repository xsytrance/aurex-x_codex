# Prime Pulse Experience

Prime Pulse is the built-in exploration experience included with Aurex.

It acts as a demonstration of the console's procedural capabilities.

Players explore the interior realm of the Prime Pulse.

The experience is heavily inspired by synesthetic games such as Rez.

Player Role

The player exists as a Resonant Explorer.

Movement is first-person and fluid rather than walking.

Players glide through the environment interacting with structures and energy nodes.

Manifest System

Players may temporarily "manifest" into energy forms tied to Prime factions.

Manifestation allows limited interaction abilities such as:

energy pulses
geometry manipulation
particle bursts

Prime Zones

The Pulse contains regions influenced by different Prime Maestros.

Each zone demonstrates a unique interaction style.

Examples:

Electronic Zone
rhythm targeting mechanics

Jazz Zone
geometry bending and improvisation

Rock Zone
shockwave interactions

Classical Zone
harmonic puzzle structures

Lore Discovery

Story fragments called Echoes are scattered throughout the Pulse.

Players discover them to piece together the history of the Prime Pulse.

Echo fragments may appear as:

audio transmissions
visual events
symbolic structures
music layers

The BassLine

Beneath the Prime Pulse lies a deep region known as the BassLine.

This realm represents the foundational rhythm of the universe.

Characteristics:

massive speaker-like structures
resonance tunnels
subharmonic bass waves

Music in this region emphasizes deep bass elements such as:

psytrance bass
funk bass
techno basslines
dub-style pulses

The BassLine is believed to be older than the Pulse itself.
